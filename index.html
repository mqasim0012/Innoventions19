About Us

Lahore Grammar School Senior Boys has, over the years, established itself as a beacon of quality education and has built up a reputation as one of the leading institutions of Pakistan. Alongside prized culture of extra and co-curricular activities, Lahore Grammar School inculcates within its students a sense of moral responsibility and social well-being. Our events have always been fervently awaited and have always been the talk of the town.



LGS JT’s Science, Mathematics, Robotics, Arts and Information Technology societies are collaborating to host a national Olympiad titled “Innoventions’19” : Pakistan’s largest school-based Science Olympiad. After being highly successful in the previous years, amassing approximately 70 teams each time, this is the institution’s 7th time hosting this event. Each year, the competition receives an unprecedented response, setting a very high benchmark for the future events in this area of co-curricular activities, and making Innoventions’19 this year’s most eagerly awaited event in the Science activity calendar.
